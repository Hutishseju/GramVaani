# GramVaani – Rural Decision Intelligence Platform
## Project by Aryavart Analytics

## 1. Problem Statement
Build an AI-powered system that supports rural ecosystems, improves market access, enhances decision-making, and promotes sustainable resource utilization using public and synthetic datasets.

## 2. Objective
To design a multilingual AI-powered platform that enables farmers and rural entrepreneurs to make data-driven decisions regarding crop pricing, weather risks, and market demand.

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Crop Price Forecasting
- Predict mandi crop prices for next 7–14 days.
- Provide trend visualization.
- Show confidence interval.

### 3.2 Weather Risk Intelligence
- Analyze historical weather patterns.
- Generate risk score (Low/Medium/High).
- Alert users about potential disruptions.

### 3.3 Demand Prediction Engine
- Identify regional demand clusters.
- Suggest optimal nearby markets.
- Predict price volatility zones.

### 3.4 Multilingual AI Advisory
- Support Hindi and regional languages.
- Provide conversational advisory.
- Explain recommendations clearly.

### 3.5 Dashboard & Alerts
- Mobile-first UI.
- Smart notification system.
- Risk indicator panel.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

- System must use public or synthetic datasets only.
- Must be scalable to multi-state level.
- Response latency under 3 seconds.
- Secure API-based architecture.
- Cloud-native deployment.

## 5. Data Requirements

- Public mandi price datasets.
- Public weather historical datasets.
- Synthetic farmer activity data.
- Government open data portals.

## 6. Assumptions

- Internet connectivity available.
- Public datasets accessible.
- Users operate mobile-first interface.

## 7. Constraints

- No use of private healthcare or restricted datasets.
- Hackathon timeline constraints.
- Prototype-level scalability.

## 8. Success Metrics

- Prediction accuracy >75%.
- User response latency <3 seconds.
- Clear advisory explanation.
- Scalability validation.
